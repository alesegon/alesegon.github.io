VIVA! Festival Video Mapping

A team composed of alumni and students from the Undergraduate Degree in Graphic Design – Motion Graphics and Fashion Design have created an experimental video mapping show that will be projected during VIVA’s closing day on the old city centre walls. A creative and innovative proposal that will bring lights and colours to Locorotondo from a contemporary and visual perspective.

This project was awarded with a Silver Laus Award’18. Viva! Festival Locorotondo Skyline Mapping See the whole experience
